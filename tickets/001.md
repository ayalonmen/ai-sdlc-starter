# Ticket 001: Swing strategy backtest

Add and remove members in a single group. Names are required and must be unique within the group. Removing a member is blocked if they appear in any expense (as payer or sharer).

---

<!-- Parse agent output gets appended below this line after approval. -->


## Tasks

- Add a UI action to create a new member within a group.
- Add a UI action to remove an existing member from a group.
- Enforce that member names are required (non-empty) at entry time.
- Enforce that member names are unique within a group (case-insensitive comparison to avoid duplicate-looking entries).
- Block member removal when the member appears in any expense in the group, either as the payer or as one of the sharers.
- Surface inline validation messages for rejected add/remove actions (empty name, duplicate name, removal blocked) without corrupting existing state.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Adding a member with a non-empty, unique (case-insensitive) name succeeds and the member appears in the group's member list.
2. Attempting to add a member with an empty or whitespace-only name is rejected and shows an inline validation message; no member is added.
3. Attempting to add a member whose name matches an existing member's name in the same group (case-insensitive, leading/trailing whitespace trimmed) is rejected and shows an inline validation message; no duplicate member is added.
4. Removing a member who does not appear as a payer or sharer in any expense in the group succeeds and the member is removed from the group's member list.
5. Attempting to remove a member who is the payer on at least one expense in the group is blocked and shows an inline validation message; the member is not removed.
6. Attempting to remove a member who is a sharer on at least one expense in the group is blocked and shows an inline validation message; the member is not removed.
7. A blocked or rejected add/remove action leaves all existing members, expenses, and balances unchanged (no partial state mutation).
8. Member list and any related balance calculations persist correctly to localStorage after a successful add or remove, and are correctly rehydrated on reload.
